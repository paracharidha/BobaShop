What is Boba Tea?

Boba originates from Taiwan where the pearls were used in popular desserts where milk tea is just as popular, until someone mixed the two! The tea base is usually black or green tea and can be useed with an array of flavored syrups. Here at K's we add milk to give our boba tea's the creamy yummy texture!
